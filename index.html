📚 What is Soldering?

Soldering joins electronic components using melted metal (solder) to form a strong electrical connection.

⚠️ Cold Solder Joint

Happens when heat is insufficient, causing weak, unreliable connections.

✅ Proper Soldering

  • Use proper temperature (~350°C)
  • Clean tip regularly
  • Heat pad + lead together
  • Apply solder to joint
  • Let it cool naturally

🧰 Materials

  • Soldering Iron
  • Solder Wire
  • Flux
  • PCB Board
  • Desoldering Pump
  • Safety Goggles
